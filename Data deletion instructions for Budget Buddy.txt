How to Request Data Deletion in Budget Buddy
At Budget Buddy, we value your privacy and provide an easy way to request the deletion of your data. Follow the steps below to permanently delete your information:

1. Open the App
Launch the Budget Buddy app on your mobile device.

2. Go to Settings
Tap on the Settings icon, usually located in the menu or at the top-right corner of the screen.

3. Select "Account"
In the Settings menu, look for the Account section and tap on it.

4. Request Data Deletion
Find the option labeled "Delete My Data" or "Request Data Deletion." Tap on it.

Important Notes:
Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
